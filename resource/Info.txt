    time: Unix timestamp (nanoseconds precision) - Exact moment of lightning strike detection
    lat: Latitude coordinate (-90 to 90) - Geographic location of strike
    lon: Longitude coordinate (-180 to 180) - Geographic location of strike
    region: Region identifier (1-14) - Geographic region classification
    mds: Minimum Detectable Signal - Signal strength measurement
    mc: Modal Count - Number of detected modes
    gs: Ground Strike indicator - Whether strike reached ground (0 or 1)
